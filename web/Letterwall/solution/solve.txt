# Letterwall Web Application


Topics: Misc,Web

Walkthrough:
 - On the main page, login as a guest
 - You will be presented with a wall of text that is constantly changing
 - Find the words that are not periodically changing and read the phrase
 - Change your guest JWT for an admin one by replacing the username
 - Reload the wall of text page with your new JWT
 - Find the words that are not periodically changing and read the flag
